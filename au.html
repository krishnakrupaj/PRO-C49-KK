ABOUT US

We are a group of origami lovers in Tirunelveli.We are a group of collage students of Manonmaniam Sundarnar University. We read more and more about the art of Origami. We wanted to show the public the beauty of origami. We also want children enjoy the art, decorate the homes, make the parents proud and they can do more.This is our own website. We will add activities, origamis and steps to do them.This is a free website and our motto is to give the art of origami to ALL. We also have a youtube channel (origami for all - youtube channel name) so that we could give you a clear ideas through videos.
